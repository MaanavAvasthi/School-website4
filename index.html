Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The church is a place of worship for christians. It is considered as a place of god. It is a holy building for christian religious activity. People gather at church and pray to god. They light candles in front of Jesus. There are two main missions of every church One mission is to develop Christ- like attributes in mens lives. Second mission is to transform society to a better peaceful place to live. There is a saint in every church called “Father”. Every year on the eve of Christmas, the church is decorated with flowers, bells etc.





School Library

Was established in 1980

The library is the heart and soul of an educational institution. Every school should have a library. ... Children should be encouraged to use the library which should be properly furnished and properly equipped with good books. There should be books for all ages and for all tastes. The books provide us with specialized knowledge, information and wisdom.



School Auditorium

Was established in 1982

Most of the school theatres and auditoriums are exclusively designed for stage shows. An effective design must have specific parameters with a brilliant space and sound system. A few of the basic requirements for any school auditorium include: Audience Chamber. The audience chamber must be big enough to hold any performance band, music, and artist.
